Feijoada

The Brazilian style feijoada is a dish consisting of a black bean stew with various types of pork and beef. It is served with farofa, white rice, braised kale and sliced orange, among other side dishes.

Kung Pao Chicken

Kung Pao Chicken, is a chinese fried and spicy dish made with chicken, peanuts, vegetables, and red pepper. It is a classic Sichuan cuisine dish that originated in Sichuan Province, southwest China and includes Sichuan black pepper.

Parmegiana

Steak Parmigiana or Filet Parmigiana, a Brazilian dish, is a type of fried steak, consisting of a piece of sliced meat, breaded with wheat flour and eggs (egg white), topped with parmesan cheese, ham and plenty tomato sauce and condiments such as oregano and cilantro to taste.
